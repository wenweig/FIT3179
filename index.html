Australian Labour Force

This dashboard presents a comprehensive analysis of the Australian workforce,

highlighting key trends and insights through interactive visualisations of employment data from 2014 to 2024.

The map visualisation displays the yearly geographical distribution of the Australian workforce across various regions, allowing for exploration of employment density, providing valuable context for understanding workforce trends.

The heatmap illustrates the average working hours per month across various regions in Australia from 2014 to 2024. This visualisation provides a more in-depth view of workforce activity, highlighting periods of peak labor demand and trends in working hours over time.

The bar chart (left) allows for comparison of the average working hours across different regions in Australia per year, providing insights into regional workforce distribution.

The bar chart (right) enables comparison of the average working hours based on employment type and gender per year, offering a breakdown of workforce participation and trends over the past decade.

The line chart provides a more in-depth analysis of workforce trends by tracking changes in average working hours by gender across various employment types over the years.
